Are Purchases Safe?

All purchases are conducted securely through Sellix, utilizing encrypted and safe platforms for selling digital goods.

How to Make a Purchase?

Making a purchase is simple. Browse our collection, add your chosen items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the easy steps to complete your purchase securely.

What is the Refund Policy?

We typically don't process refunds once a key has been activated. However, if you encounter technical issues or the game doesn't work as expected, we may consider a refund. To initiate the refund process, please submit a support ticket on our Discord server and provide video proof of the issue. Refunds are not applicable for change of mind or if the game doesn't meet personal preferences. Please review your order carefully before completing the purchase. For more details, refer to our Terms of Service.

How to Contact Support?

Support is only offered through our Discord. Join our Discord server and reach out to our support team for assistance with any concerns or queries.

Can I Change My Order?

Unfortunately, once an order is placed, it cannot be changed. Please review your order carefully before completing the purchase.

What Platforms are Supported?

We exclusively support PC as our gaming platform. Check our product descriptions for compatibility with PC.

Do You Offer Game Demos?

We do not offer game demos for OBS reasons. Stay tuned for promotional events where you may have opportunities to try out new games.

How to Redeem Game Codes?

Redeeming game codes is simple. Follow the instructions provided with your purchase to activate and enjoy your new game.

Are Digital Copies the Same as Physical Copies?

Yes, digital copies of games offer the same content as physical copies. The only difference is the delivery method.

Can I Suggest Games for Your Collection?

Absolutely! We value your suggestions. Suggest games you would love through our Discord, and we'll consider adding them to our collection.
